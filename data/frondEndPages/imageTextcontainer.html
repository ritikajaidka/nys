Disciple of Srila Prabhupada

Meeting with Srila Prabhuapda.

*

First Meeting

He came to Delhi where one day in a marketplace he was quite impressed to see some foreign devotees distributing Krishna books. He followed them and reached the Hare Krishna temple at Palam. Next day by the Divine arrangement of Krishna he left for Vrindavan and finally reached in the Divine association of His Divine Grace A. C. Bhaktivedanta Srila Prabhupada.

*

Initiation

It was in the year 1974 when his holiness got initiation from His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, the founder Acharya of Great ISKCON and formally became his disciple.

*

Sannyas diksha

Seeing the dedication, sincerity, renunciation and pure devotion in him, Srila Prabhupada awarded him the renounced (Sannyas diksha) at the young age of 28. After taking Sannyas, His Holiness Nava Yogendra Swami Ji traveled around the world over a dozen times for preaching purposes.

Atma Series

Carrying out the order of Srila Prabhupada with extreme vigour Maharaj is still preaching strongly all over the world till date. Maharaj Ji is expert in explaining Vedic Philosophy to the masses in Hindi, English, Urdu, Dogri, Punjabi, Gujrati and few African languages too. He preaches the teachings of Lord Chaitanya through simple discourses and melodies bhajans in the same way as the great vaishnavas like Srila Bhakti Vinod Thakur, Srila Narrotama Das Thakur, Srila Lochana das Thakur did, which is easily understood and followed by common masses.
